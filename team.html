Our Journey

The Course Experience

The Discovery Vacuum

During the initial weeks of this course, we were hit with a startling realization: while everyone was teaching us *how* to use AI, no one was teaching us how to protect our intellectual identity. We spent many late nights at Mahindra University debating the "Zero-Knowledge" paradox, which eventually became the seed for Spark Mind.

The 6-3-5 Pressure Cooker

Our 6-3-5 brainstorming session was a turning point. Initially, we were stuck on simple detection tools. However, the rapid pace of the 6-3-5 method forced us to think deeper, leading us to pivot from "Detection" to "Proactive Interception." It was the most intense 30 minutes of our design journey, yielding over 100 raw concepts.

From Persona to Prototype

Interviewing stakeholders revealed a deep-seated fear of "Algorithmic Permanent Storage." One student mentioned they felt like their "brain was being harvested" by LLMs. This emotional insight shifted our prototype's focus toward the Privacy Scrubber—making data protection visible, interactive, and immediate.

Final Reflections

This project wasn't just about building a website; it was about defining a new standard for student-AI interaction. We've learned that in the age of generative intelligence, the most valuable asset isn't the AI output—it's the integrity of the original human thought.
